Have you ever wondered what fantasy heroes and heroines do on their days off?

In Celestium Life, you can find out! Enter the Celestium, an interplanetary fantasy realm where day-to-day joys abound; buy your first home in an elven capitol. Take a vacation to enchanted glades; grocery shop in the heart of giant's land, or perhaps simply travel around, exploring the world. Go to work, go to school, and upgrade your fashions.

Take your creativity and love for fantasy a step further! The Celestium awaits...
